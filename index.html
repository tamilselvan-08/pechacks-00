About

Welcome Innovators

Welcome to PEC HACKS — where innovation meets impact.

Organized by Panimalar Engineering College, Chennai, Tamil Nadu — your gateway to a world of possibilities, where passionate individuals come together to unleash their creativity, solve real-world challenges, and make a meaningful difference. As one of Tamil Nadu's leading student hackathons, PEC Hacks 4.0 is a free, 36-hour hackathon open to engineering and technology students across India, with tracks spanning AI, Web3, HealthTech, FinTech, and more.

No sleep. No limits. Just you, your team, and an idea that could change everything.

PEC Hacks is Tamil Nadu's premier student hackathon, organized by Panimalar Engineering College (PEC), Chennai. PEC Hacks 4.0 is a 36-hour international hackathon where students from across India code, collaborate, and compete to build innovative tech solutions.

PEC Hacks 4.0 runs August 29-30, 2026, a 36-hour non-stop hackathon at Panimalar Engineering College, Bangalore Trunk Road, Nazarathpettai, Poonamallee, Chennai, Tamil Nadu, India - 600123.

PEC Hacks 4.0 covers Artificial Intelligence (AI), Web3 & Blockchain, HealthTech, FinTech, EdTech, Sustainability, and Open Innovation - encouraging creative tech solutions for real-world problems.
